Privacy Policy

Data Collection

Form Saver collects and stores the following information locally on your device:

  • Form field values you enter
  • Form field types and names
  • Website URLs where forms are filled
  • Timestamps of form entries

Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's storage API. No data is transmitted to external servers.

Data Retention

The extension keeps only the 15 most recent form entries. Older entries are automatically deleted.

Your Rights

You can:

  • Clear all saved data at any time using the extension popup
  • Uninstall the extension to remove all stored data

Updates

This privacy policy may be updated. Please check back periodically.
