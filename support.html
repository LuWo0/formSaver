Installation

  1. Visit the Chrome Web Store page
  2. Click "Add to Chrome"
  3. Click "Add extension" in the popup

Usage Guide

  1. Fill out any form on any website
  2. Data is automatically saved as you type
  3. Click the extension icon to view saved data
  4. Use the "Clear All" button to remove saved data

FAQ

How many entries are saved?

The extension keeps the 15 most recent entries.

Is my data secure?

All data is stored locally on your device only.

How do I delete my data?

Use the "Clear All" button in the extension popup.
